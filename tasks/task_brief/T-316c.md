## Deliverables

- Production Ed25519 `SopTemplateSigner` and `SopTemplateVerifier` adapters with a cryptographic identity separate from profile, decision-record, and audit-HMAC keys.
- `SigningKeyArchiveStore` support for the `sop_template` purpose.
- SOP-template signing-key distribution, rotation, and revocation service emitting governance events with embedded signed decision-record evidence.
- Offline SOP-template JSON signature verifier and runbook.
- Regression tests covering round trip, tamper, wrong key version, revoked key, offline verifier parity, and key-purpose separation.
