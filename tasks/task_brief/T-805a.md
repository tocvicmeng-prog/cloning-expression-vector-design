## Scope

Implement the Phase 8a pre-screening `DraftDesignBundle` orchestration service.

- Dispatch to `engine.design_plan` for always-renderable design-plan generation/rendering.
- Dispatch to `engine.controls` for `ControlSet` generation and validation.
- Dispatch to `engine.risk_classification` for `RiskAdvisoryReport` generation.
- Emit `DesignRealisationPlanRendered`, `ControlSetRendered`, and `RiskAdvisoryReportRendered` design-stream events through an injected event log.
- Keep the bundle structurally incapable of carrying gated operational protocol artefacts, screening verdicts, vendor submission clearance, or operational authorisation evidence.
